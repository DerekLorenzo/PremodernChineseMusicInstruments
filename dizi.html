Dizi

Dizi is a bamboo flute with a rich history in Chinese culture.

Typically made of bamboo, the dizi is a rather simple instrument. However, it has a rich history as part of premodern Chinese music. It has versatile uses, but is often used in Chinese folk music, opera, and modern orchestra.

Though the dizi is frequently made of bamboo, it is occasionally made from other wood or even stone. A unique feature of the dizi is a special membrane used to create a resonating effect. A hole is glued over with reed that has undergone a special process to create the membrane.
