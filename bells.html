Bells

Bronze bells(Bianzhong) is an ancient set of bells played in traditional Chinese music.

Hover mouse over flip card

The bells were designed to produce separate notes when struck from the center or from either side.

Bells have a long and storied history as a part of Chinese music and ritual. Additionally, there are many different kinds of bells, starting from some of the earliest bells such as the taozhong to the bianzhong displayed on this page. Though different regions and time periods produced slightly different variations of the bell set, the usage of bells was common throughout premodern China. They were commonplace in temples and individuals in power sought to own sets of bells as a symbol of their status.
